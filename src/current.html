Ready to revolutionize your plants?

Grow Lush & Healthy Plants with Less Fertilizer

Amani Ag presents M2 MicroTM, a revolutionary solution that is changing the face of agriculture. This product, infused with Soil Catalyst TechnologyTM, gives farmers the power to produce healthy, high-yield crops while reducing their water and fertilizer usage. The results speak for themselves:

– 40+% reduction in overall water use

– 50+% reduction in NPK fertilizer requirements

– Increased growth rates leading to earlier harvests and increased overall yearly production

– Increased alfalfa quality and plant health including near complete reduction in insect pests without the use of pesticides

M2 MicroTM is available in a convenient liquid concentrate that is easily incorporated into irrigation systems or applied as a spray. One gallon of concentrate treats 10 acres of land, greatly reducing handling challenges and making it accessible to all farmers.

Our Philosophy

At Amani Ag, our mission is to develop and manufacture products from natural sources to enhance and supplement agricultural land resulting in increased crop production, improved economic value for farmers, better health for consumers, and a more sustainable environment.
Our vision is to create a brighter future for farming families and businesses, through increased and sustainable agricultural production that provides stability for the larger markets they serve.

M2 MicroTM can positively transform agricultural economics and environmental sustainability

40+% Reduction in Irrigation Requirements

As droughts continue to ravage the American Southwest and West, M2 MicroTM is a lifeline for farmers, providing a 40+% reduction in irrigation requirements and maintaining agricultural viability even during the toughest conditions.

Substantial Reduction of Industrial Fertilizer

With increased energy prices and global supply instability NPK fertilizers have seen dramatic price increases. Additionally, environmental concerns of ground and surface water pollution from NPK fertilizers raises further concerns of long-term agricultural sustainability. M2 MicroTM provides immediate, realistic and effective answers to these problems.

While much of the initial testing and use of M2 MicroTM has focused on irrigated alfalfa and grass, the applications for the product extend far beyond and include such crops as barley, wheat, soybeans, corn and all types of vegetables. M2 MicroTM is currently available through Authorized Resellers and Distributors with support from Amani Ag’s Customer Service and Development teams.

About Us

Drs. Bryon Tarbet (Ph.D. Organic Chemistry) and Heather Tarbet (Ph.D. Biochemistry) lead Amani Ag’s Research & Development team, focused on developing natural products suitable for large-scale agriculture. Their expertise in the rhizosphere – the zone of soil surrounding a plant root where soil microbiology and chemistry interact with the root – has allowed them to create the proprietary Soil Catalyst TechnologyTM now incorporated into the M2 MicroTM product. With M2 MicroTM, Amani Ag introduces targeted microbiology and nutrients into soil, turbocharging the rhizosphere to enhance the natural growth and development of plants.
